Translation & AI
How accurate are the translations?
Translations are generated using Google ML Kit AI. While highly advanced, they may not be 100% accurate. Always verify sensitive or legal information before relying on the app.
Why is a specific language not working?
Ensure both source and target language models are downloaded in Settings > Language & Translation. If the model download fails, check your internet connection and device storage.
Voice & Connectivity
Speech-to-Text (STT) Requirements Technical
Voice input accuracy depends on your device's speech engine. While the translation is offline, Android's voice recognition may occasionally require internet access to process audio clearly.
Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Troubleshooting
For local voice chat, both devices must have Bluetooth and Location enabled. On Android 12+, "Nearby Devices" permission is mandatory. P2P works within a 10-15 meter range.
Privacy & Security
Is my voice being recorded?
No. We prioritize your privacy. Audio is processed locally on your device for translation and then immediately discarded. CLVCA does not store voice recordings on any server.
How can I delete my data?
You can wipe all translation history and cached settings at any time by going to Settings > Support > Clear App Data. This action is permanent.
Account & Ads
Why am I seeing advertisements?
To keep CLVCA free for everyone, we use Google AdMob to show ads. These partners may use your Device ID or Advertising ID to provide relevant content.
Ad-blocking software is prohibited as per our Terms & Conditions.
