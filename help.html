CLVCA (Cross-Language Voice Chat Application) is an offline AI-powered communication app designed for real-time translation between nearby devices.

Voice Recognition & Output

Understand how CLVCA processes your speech and generates audio.

  • Speech-to-Text (STT): Translation works 100% offline, but high-accuracy voice recognition depends on your device's native engine. Some devices may require a one-time internet connection to verify voice data.
  • Silent Output (TTS): CLVCA uses the Flutter TTS engine. If you hear no sound, please ensure "Speech Services by Google" is set as your default engine in your Phone Settings.
  • Regional Accents: You can customize speaking speed, pitch, and regional accents in Settings > Voice & Audio.

P2P Connectivity

Connect with nearby friends without using cellular data or the internet.

  • How to Link: One user must "Start Hosting" while the other selects "Join Friend."
  • Scanning QR: For instant pairing, ask your friend to scan the QR code displayed on the Host's screen.
  • Effective Range: Maintain a distance of 10-15 meters for a stable Bluetooth/Wi-Fi connection.

Privacy Promise

Your privacy is built into the core of CLVCA.

  • On-Device Processing: CLVCA does not upload your personal voice data to any server. All processing happens locally on your hardware.
  • History Control: You can permanently delete your conversation logs by selecting "Clear All History" in the History screen.
  • Cache Reset: Logging out or clearing app data in Settings will reset your configurations and temporary cache.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app work without internet?

A: Yes, as long as you have downloaded the required offline language models.

Q: Does the app show advertisements?

A: Free users may see limited ads to support the maintenance of the app.

Q: My voice is not being detected?

A: Ensure you have granted Microphone permissions and speak clearly near the device.

Troubleshooting

  • Language Model Download Issues: If offline language models are not downloading, paused, or stuck:
    • Connect to a stable Wi-Fi network and retry.
    • Ensure enough free storage is available.
    • Disable Battery Saver or Data Saver temporarily.
    • Keep CLVCA open while the download completes.
    • Restart the app if progress does not continue.
    • Restart your device and try again later if needed.
  • Connection Failed: Ensure both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled on both devices.
  • No Audio: Check your device's media volume and ensure the TTS engine is updated.
  • App Crash: Restart the application or clear the app cache from settings.
  • Storage Issues: Ensure you have enough free space (approx. 50MB per language) to download models.
