Find quick answers to common questions about using CLVCA for offline voice translation.

Voice & Audio

Troubleshoot speech recognition and audio playback.

  • Why is the translation silent (TTS)? CLVCA uses Flutter TTS, which depends on your phone's native speech engine. If you can't hear anything, ensure the "Voice Data" for that language is installed in Settings > Google TTS Output.
  • Does Speech-to-Text (STT) need internet? In most cases, yes. Accuracy depends on your device's engine. While translation is offline, your phone may need a connection for high-accuracy voice processing.
  • How to change Voice speed? You can adjust the pitch and speech rate directly from Settings > Voice & Audio.

Managing Your Data

You have full control over what is stored on your device.

  • How to delete History? Go to the Translation History screen. You can delete individual logs or clear the entire list from there.
  • How to remove Language Models? Navigate to Settings > Manage Languages. You can uninstall any downloaded model to free up space.
  • How to clear App Cache? Logging out from the Settings menu will automatically clear your temporary app cache and user configurations.

P2P & Connection

Chat with nearby friends without using cellular data.

  • How to use P2P? Ensure Bluetooth and Location are ON. One person must "Start Host" and the other "Join". Range is approx. 10-15 meters.
  • Why sign in? Guest mode works locally, but signing in with Google allows you to sync your preferences and access premium features.

Accuracy Disclaimer

Important: AI translations are not always 100% accurate. Please verify critical information before relying on them for legal or medical purposes.
